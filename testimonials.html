Testimonials

Due to the nature of the procedure, our success is marked by our client's lack of knowledge of the memory erasure. In lieu of their recommendations, we can provide testimonials from the loved ones of clients who have seen first-hand the positive impacts the procedure has had. Please contact us if you would like to contact a reference directly.

She got her job and her friends back, and has no memory of the horrible relaitonship. I would recommend Lacuna to anyone who may need it." - Mary Billings
